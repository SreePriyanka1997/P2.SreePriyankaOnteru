Car DashBoard

Examples of Car Dashboards

1.Audi Sport Quatttro 2014



Pros:

  1. The Design is simple
  2. The Dashboard didn't occupy much space

Cons:

  1. The main focus is on tachometer
  2. The warning alerts are not clear
  3. The fuel guage display is not obvious
  4. Overall the dashboard more distracting

2.KIA K900 2015



Pros:

  1. Visibility of speedometer is good enough
  2. Chances of driver getting distracted is very less
  3. Fuel Guage and Engine Temperature Guage are also clearly visible

Cons:

  1. Both the speedometer and tachometer designs are similar which is hard to differentiate in a glance

3.Lamborghini Aventador



Pros:

  1. Multiple options on the screen
  2. Visibility of all the guage bars is good

Cons:

  1. Main Focus is on tachometer
  2. Warning alerts are displayed all over the screen
  3. Seems to be more distracting

4.Porsche Taycan



Pros:

  1. Visibility of speedometer is good
  2. Displays the outside temperature and time

Cons:

  1. Essential warning alerts are missing
  2. Infotainment display on the dashboard leads to distraction

5.Honda Accord - 2018



Pros:

  1. The size of speedometer and tachometer is good
  2. Fuel and temperature guage bars are clear

Cons:

  1. Space is not utilized properly
  2. Warning alerts are located in a small amount of space

Common Uses

  1. Tracking the speed
  2. Checking the fuel guage and refill accordingly
  3. Turn Signals are also most commonly used
  4. Safety notifications like seat belt, door lock etc.
  5. Checking warnings when pop-up

Rare Uses

  1. Checking RPM
  2. Checking outside current temperature
  3. Unusual warning alerts
  4. Checking Odometer
  5. Hands free device control

Controls that User Interacts

  1. Wipers
  2. Turn Signals
  3. Light Beams
  4. Cruise Control
  5. Resetting the Odometer

Common Mistakes

  1. Getting confused between speedometer and tachometer
  2. Considering warning alerts as an emergency issue
  3. Getting confused between kmph and mph

Design Improvement

The dashboard should be clear. It shouldnt distract the driver.It is recommended to display warnings of less imapact in a separate section. The speedometer should be smaller when compared to tachometer as the drivers focus should be mostly confined to speedometer.
